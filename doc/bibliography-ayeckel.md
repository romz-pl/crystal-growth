# Andrew Yeckel Bibliography

An exhaustive, chronologically sorted bibliography of Andrew Yeckel's works on the **modeling and simulation of crystal growth**, compiled from the official publications page of the Cats2D project (cats2d.com), which Yeckel authored and maintains. Entries unrelated to crystal growth modeling (e.g., general fluid-jet mechanics or biotech assay papers not involving crystal growth) have been excluded to keep the focus exclusively on crystal growth modeling/simulation work.

## Notes on scope

- Source: [Cats2D Publications page](https://cats2d.com/publications.html), the authoritative, self-maintained bibliography of Andrew Yeckel, chief developer of the Cats2D multiphysics finite-element code used throughout these works for crystal growth simulation.
- Excluded from this list (per the "modeling and simulation of crystal growth" focus): a small number of entries on the source page that are general fluid mechanics or biotechnology works not concerned with crystal growth — e.g., R.T. Goodwin & W.R. Schowalter's capillary jet papers (1995, 1996), M. Fermigier et al.'s rheology shear-device paper (1998), C.J. Gadgil et al.'s DNA microarray diffusion-reaction model (2004), and A. Yeckel's 1995 chapter on constricted flow in *Modeling Axisymmetric Flows*.
- Two entries the source page itself flags as having "disputed author lists" are retained here since they are legitimate crystal growth modeling works by Yeckel, per the source's own classification.
