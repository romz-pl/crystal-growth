# Bibliography: Peer-Reviewed Journal Articles on FEMAG / FEMAG-CZ (FEMAGSoft S.A., Belgium)

This bibliography compiles the peer-reviewed journal articles describing the FEMAG software family (including FEMAG-CZ, the Czochralski-process module) for crystal growth simulation, as listed in the official "Journal Papers" section of FEMAGSoft S.A.'s publications page. Entries are sorted chronologically (oldest to newest). Note that the earliest entries predate the formal incorporation of FEMAGSoft S.A. and describe the underlying global-simulation methodology (developed principally by F. Dupret and collaborators at Université catholique de Louvain) that was subsequently commercialized as the FEMAG product line.
