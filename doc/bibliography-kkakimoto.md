# Koichi Kakimoto (Tohoku University, Sendai, Japan) Bibliography

This bibliography compiles publications related to crystal growth research associated with **Koichi Kakimoto**, currently Professor at the New Industry Creation Hatchery Center, **Tohoku University**, Sendai, Japan (previously Kyushu University, Research Institute for Applied Mechanics, 1996–2021/2022; NEC Fundamental Research Laboratories, 1985–1996). Entries are organized into **Experimental Work** and **Modeling & Simulation Work**, and sorted chronologically within each section. Some works combine both experimental validation and numerical modeling; these are cross-referenced where relevant.

- The **modeling and simulation** category emphasizes global furnace models (coupled heat transfer, melt convection, magnetohydrodynamics), dislocation-density modeling via the Alexander–Haasen(–Sumino) framework, impurity (oxygen, carbon, nitrogen, phosphorus) transport modeling, and first-principles/DFT and thermodynamic analyses of III-nitride vapor-phase epitaxy.
- Several entries (e.g., the OCCC β-Ga₂O₃ growth papers, the global CZ modeling series) exist in both preprint and peer-reviewed journal form; both are listed where identified, since dates differ and either may be the version a reader encounters.
- This list, while extensive, reflects publicly indexed records as of July 2026 and should not be considered absolutely complete given the size of Kakimoto's total output (~398 publications per ResearchGate).
